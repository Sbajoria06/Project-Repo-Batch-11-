Occasionally, indeed, when such a consummation seemed inevitable, a watchful old grandam with her close cap, disstaff, and spindle, rushed like a sibil in frenzy out of one of these miserable cells, dashed into the middle of the path, and snatching up her own charge from among the sun-burnt loiterers, saluted him with a sound cuff, and transported him back to his dungeon. The little white-headed, violet screaming all the while from the very top of his lungs a shrilly, trebled to the growling remonstrances of the enraged matron.
